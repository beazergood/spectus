Since 1957

Spectus Energy has been at the forefront of combustion engineering for over six decades. We pioneered suspended flame technology, self-cooling tip shut-off fuel injection systems, and advanced flame monitoring — developing eleven generations of detection systems with over 3,000 units deployed worldwide.

Our equipment is installed in power stations across the UK, Australia, China, and Hong Kong, including Drax — Europe's largest coal-fired power station — in partnership with Babcock, Doosan, Foster Wheeler, and Hitachi.
